Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tourist Trap or Not.

How accurate are your ratings?

Our ratings are based on a combination of factors including:

  • Price comparison - How prices compare to similar local establishments
  • Review analysis - Sentiment analysis from multiple review platforms
  • Local input - Contributions from verified local residents
  • Transparency - Menu pricing clarity and hidden fees

While no system is perfect, our algorithm has been shown to be 92% accurate when compared to expert travel recommendations.

Can I contribute my own reviews?

Absolutely! We encourage all users to contribute their experiences. Here's how you can help:

  • Rate places you've visited with our simple 5-star system
  • Write detailed reviews about your experience
  • Upload photos of the food, menu prices, and atmosphere
  • Verify information about existing listings

Local residents can apply for Verified Local status which gives their reviews additional weight in our algorithm.

How do you prevent fake reviews?

We take review authenticity very seriously and employ multiple safeguards:

  • Verified visits - We confirm users have actually been to locations through location verification
  • AI detection - Our algorithms detect patterns of fake or paid reviews
